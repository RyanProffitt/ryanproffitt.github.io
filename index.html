About Me

Ryan Proffit is a Software Engineer living in Tokyo, Japan.

After interning at NASA, Ryan moved to Japan to work for Astroscale, an aerospace company specializing in clearing Earth-orbit of debris and providing on-orbit services to spacecraft.

He is actively involved in the development of vehicles capable of providing End-of-Life and Active Debris Removal services.
